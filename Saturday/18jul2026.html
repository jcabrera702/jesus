Who Do You Say That I Am?

July 18, 2026

Generative Summary

God spoke through prophets and now through His Son, Jesus, who is superior to angels, creator, and sustainer of the universe, seated at God's right hand. Believers are urged to heed salvation, live by faith, and recognize their new identity in Christ, emphasizing grace, righteousness, and reconciliation with God through faith and the Holy Spirit.
